ABOUT

Welcome to Music Together Melody!

Music Together® is one of the leading early childhood education programs recognized around the world. Designed for babies to kindergartners and their parents, the classes help promote family enrichment through music. Music Together Melody has been serving the Montgomery county area for 10 years led by instructor Marianna Wise, who has been working with children for over 22 years. Classes are offered in D.C., North Bethesda and Germantown, MD.

Marianna H. Wise

Music Together® is an internationally recognized early childhood music program for babies, toddlers, preschoolers, kindergarteners, and the adults who love them. First offered to the public in 1987, it pioneered the concept of a research-based, developmentally appropriate early childhood music curriculum that strongly emphasizes and facilitates adult involvement. All classes are based on the recognition that all children are musical. All children can learn to sing in tune, keep a beat, and participate with confidence in the music of our culture, provided that their early environment supports such learning. By emphasizing actual music experiences rather than concepts about music, Music Together introduces children to the pleasures of making music instead of passively receiving it from CDs or TV.

Meet The Instructor

Marianna H. Wise

My name is Marianna Wise and I have been teaching Music Together classes since 2005. I received my BA in early childhood education, double majored in child psychology and music. It has always been my dream to teach and educate children. Music has been a part of my life since childhood.  Growing up in Hungary I attended a special music school, where I learned trained on the piano for 10 years. I performed regularly in concerts and accompanied the chorus. I took that passion for music with me as I continued my education and moved into the professional field. Now I have 22 years of experience working with children from infancy to kindergarten, and I have enjoyed every moment of my career. I have worked as both a lead teacher and assistant director in Bethesda for many years and also at the World Bank Children’s Center in Washington D.C. 

I joined the Music Together® Staff in June 2005 after successfully completing the training with the Center for Music and Young Children in Princeton, NJ.  I teach in the both the preschool setting and parent-children classes as well. Music plays a big part in my life and I am exited to share and teach children and their families every day.
